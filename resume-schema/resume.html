About Chrissy:

Christine Jones is a digital communications professional specializing in content strategy, web management, and user-centered design. As Academic Editor and Web Manager at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock, she leads departmental web initiatives, oversees academic catalog publishing, and develops strategic digital messaging across all platforms.

Holding a Bachelors in Public Relations, a Masters in Professional & Technical Writing, and a forthcoming Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Web Design and Development (Spring 2027), Christine combines editorial expertise with technical fluency in front-end development, SEO, and content management systems. Her approach bridges strategy and execution, ensuring digital communications are clear, accessible, and aligned with organizational objectives.
