About VXV Events

VXV EVENTS, LLC is a Mississippi based company producing multi-genre pop culture conventions across the Mid-West and Mid-South, with plans to expand further across the United States.


Comic Con events include celebrities from franchises such as Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, SpongeBob Squarepants, Batman, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Mighty Morphin Power Rangers, and from iconic brands such as the WWE Hall of Fame, Marvel, DC, and more. Anime Fests feature voice actors and guests from popular series such as Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Dragonball, My Hero Academia, and many more.


The True Translation of VXV:


“Vi veri veniversum vivus vici.”


"By the power of truth, I, while living, have conquered the universe."

Our Commitment to Our Volunteers:


VXV Events has been organizing premier fan conventions for over a decade. Our events bring together fans from all walks of life to celebrate their favorite shows, movies, comics, and games.

Our volunteer crew is the backbone of every successful event. From registration to guest services, from stage management to crowd coordination, our volunteers make the magic happen.

We believe in creating a supportive, inclusive environment where everyone can contribute their unique skills and passions. Whether you're a first-time volunteer or a seasoned con veteran, there's a place for you on our team!
